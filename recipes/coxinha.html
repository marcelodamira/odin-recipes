Coxinha

Ingredients

For the mass

  1. 600ml of water
  2. Chicken broth
  3. Margarine
  4. 1kg flour
  5. Milk to coat
  6. Breadcrumbs to coat

For the filling

  1. 15 ml of soy oil
  2. Half onion
  3. One garlic clove
  4. 1kg of chicken breast cooked and shredded
  5. Tomato sauce
  6. Salt
  7. Parsley and chive to speckle

Steps

Mass

  • Boil the water with the chicken broth and margarine
  • Put all the flour and mix it hard, when the flour unglue turn off the fire and let it cool down
  • Grase your hands in soy oil, take small portions of the mass, fill it and model it like coxinhas
  • Then coat them first in the milk and then in the breadcrumbs
  • Deep frie it and voil√°

Filling

  • Saute all the igredients, except the chicken
  • Add the chicken and let it cook
  • Speckle the parsley and chive
