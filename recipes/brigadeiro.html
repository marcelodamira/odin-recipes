Brigadeiro

Ingredients

  • 01 Condensed milk
  • 50g of Chocolate Powder
  • 50g of butter
  • Granulated chocolate

Steps

  1. Put the condensen milk, chocolate powder and the butter in a pan
  2. Then heat it up, always mixing until it unglue (abou 10 minutes)
  3. Remove it from the fire, put on another bowl and let it cool down
  4. With a spoom take small pieces and make a ball, then pass the balls in the granulated chocolate
